MULTISTAGE PUMPS




How it works

Multistage pumps are a type of centrifugal pump designed to generate high pressure by using multiple impellers (stages) within a single pump casing. Each impeller increases the pressure of the fluid, making these pumps ideal for applications that require high discharge pressure with relatively low power consumption. They are widely used in industries, commercial applications, and high-rise buildings for efficient water supply and fluid transfer.


Standard Features
  • Ideal for industrial processes, water supply, and high-pressure applications
  • Compact and efficient design for easy installation
  • High flow rate with stable pressure output
  • Space-saving design, perfect for high-rise buildings and boiler feed systems
  • Corrosion-resistant construction for long-term durability
  • Low noise and vibration for smooth performance
