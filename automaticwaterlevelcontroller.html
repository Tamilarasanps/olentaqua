AUTOMATIC WATER LEVEL CONTROLLER




Our Automatic Water Level Controllers provide a hassle-free solution to manage water levels efficiently, saving water, electricity, and time. Designed for residential, commercial, and industrial use, our controllers ensure your tanks are always optimally filled.

How it works

Automatic Water Level Controller is a unique product of Olent Aqua Devices (P) Ltd. It is manufactured duly considering the requirements & demands of various customers. It is a completely automatic system with built in advanced" pump set protection circuitry.


Salient Features
  • Aqua Pride will switch On / Off your pump automatically.
  • Switches Off the pump automatically if the top tank full & sump is empty.
  • Switches Off the pump during high Voltage & low voltage conditions.
  • The system also provides Dry Run & Overload protection to the pump set (i.e when water is not being pumped into overhead tank due to any reason) only in Platina model.
  • Hi-tech non contact type water proof Olent Float switch to avoid regular maintenance protect you from salt water & electric shock.
  • Shows clear indication of water levels in the overhead tank & sump.
  • The System is adoptable for any kind of starters for both single phase & three phase water pumps.
  • Auto Manual switch is provided to avoid inconvenience.
  • Aqua Pride has been categorized in various models such as Pearl, Silver, Platina according to your pumping needs.
